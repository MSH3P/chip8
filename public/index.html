Just Another Chip8 Emulator
What is chip8?

Chip8 is an interpreted language that was developed in the mid 70's

programs are run on a Chip8 Virtual machine. It was made to allow easier development of

video games on 8bit microcomputers like the Cosmac VIP and Telmac 1800.

Due to it's simplicity it's a good entry into learning about emulation, binary numbers, etc...
